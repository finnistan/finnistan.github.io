Ajcan Dexceimber 11, 2021, at 13:00, Finn Putnam o ceix savster karalijašt ðaf Détsedésati vo Nieheŋkieciep vo ðaf Empfeir vo Finnistán. Peloþól ðaf nasjon ofietsjelce détsedésašt nieheŋkieciep ajcan Dexceimber 11, ðaf cetesienciep žarvef vax emëjan zon Dexceimber 6, 2021.

Laftötsu kruntets ðaf Empfeir vo Finnistán, Impefeičer Finn vax ain zeitsǝ vo Fuzzbertia zon nilantsace njox iereix kruntets en 2018. Peloþól Finn vax pekersaŋiec axeraŋg Fuzzbertia en 2018 o nilantsace entsu 2019, Finn steixanerašt niejaktif en ðaf nasjon njox 2019. En 2020, deulets ðaf COVID-19 eldemik, Finn kruntašt ðaf Könǝciep vo Sifoland, ain nilantsace lextašt nasjon daits naišen vevénašt bvejeb fiejel cetesieno þön Finn ceimzels, o vax deškrübašt norǝ voxo njox.

Ðaf vënör mikronasjon Finn vax igovalce ain zeitsǝ vo vör Finnistán's kruntets vax ðaf Uviet Sonion. Kruntašt en Oktober 2021, ðaf Uviet Sonion vax ain elšaficmojict mikronasjon en Üpstet Novo Jork, o vax ðaf fjivöro tsu ðaf Moskau Federalti (now known as the Hudson Federation). The Uviet Sonion, throughout its entire existence, was at war with the ACR (Anti-Communist Rebellion) led by Jackson's brother. When Finnistan was founded ajcan Dexceimber 11, 2021, the Uviet Sonion threatened to attack and annex the Empire. Finn was aided in creating a plan for war by the ACR and Fuzzbertia, however, by the end of the day, the Uviet Sonion took back the threats, and offered an alliance rather than a rivalry.
